Secure Shopping

At AURA, your security is our priority. We use industry-standard encryption (SSL) to protect your data during every transaction.

  • Payment Protection: We do not store your full card details. All payments are processed through PCI-DSS compliant gateways.
  • Data Encryption: Your personal information is encrypted at rest and in transit.
  • Fraud Prevention: Our automated systems monitor for suspicious activity to keep your account safe.

Authenticity Guarantee

We take pride in the quality and origin of every item in our collection. AURA guarantees that all products sold on our platform are 100% authentic.

Each product undergoes a rigorous multi-point inspection before it reaches your doorstep. We work directly with designers and certified distributors to ensure the highest standards.

Easy Returns

If your purchase doesn't perfectly match your aura, we're here to help. Our 30-day "No-Questions-Asked" return policy ensures a stress-free experience.

  • Returns: Initiate a return within 30 days of delivery.
  • Condition: Items must be in their original packaging with tags intact.
  • Refunds: Once received, refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Shipping Policy

Standard delivery typically takes 3-5 business days across major cities. Express shipping options are available at checkout for those who can't wait.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is sacred. We only collect data necessary to provide you with a personalized and efficient shopping experience.
